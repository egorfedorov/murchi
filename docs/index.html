Get Started in Seconds

No account needed. No internet required.

1
Download Murchi.dmg
2
Open the DMG and drag Murchi to Applications
3
If macOS blocks it: open Terminal, run xattr -cr /Applications/Murchi.app
4
Your pet appears on the desktop! Cmd+Shift+M to summon
